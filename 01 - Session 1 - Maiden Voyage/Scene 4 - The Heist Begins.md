[]()# Scene 4 — The Heist Begins

## Scene Purpose
- Transition from social to action
- Introduce [[Captain Ash]] and his crew as competent, sympathetic threats
- Establish that the players are caught in the crossfire, not the target
- Create moral ambiguity: [[The Raiders]] are not cartoon villains

## Trigger
Approximately four hours into the journey, as the train passes through the rolling hills of central Thrane. Dinner has ended. Most passengers are in their cabins or the lounge.

**First sign:** The lights flicker. The music stops. The conductor stones' hum changes pitch — lower, angrier.

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **מפעיל:** כ-4 שעות לתוך הנסיעה, כשהרכבת עוברת דרך הגבעות המתגלגלות של ת'ריין המרכזית. הארוחה הסתיימה. רוב הנוסעים בבתיהם או במסעדה.
> **סימן ראשון:** האורות מהבהבים. המוזיקה נעצרת. המהום של אבני המוליך משתנה בגובה — נמוך יותר, זועם יותר.

## The Attack
From the roof and through the cargo car windows, ropes descend. Figures in dark tactical gear drop with military precision.
- **6 [[The Raiders|Raiders]]** breach the train
- **2** secure the engine car
- **2** head for the cargo car
- **2** fan out to control passengers

The players are in the dining car when the first explosion rocks the train — not enough to derail, but enough to shatter windows and throw everyone off balance.

## [[Captain Ash]]'s Entrance
Through the smoke and rain entering through broken windows, **[[Captain Ash]]** steps into the dining car. His arcane goggles reflect the emergency lights. His voice is calm — almost gentle.

**Read Aloud:**
> *"Nobody needs to die today. We're here for what's in the cargo car. [[House Orien]] has been moving ancient weapons across [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]] under the guise of unity. We're here to stop that. Stay down. Stay quiet. And when this is over, tell your children you saw something worth remembering."*

**Hebrew:**
> *"אף אחד לא צריך למות היום. אנחנו כאן בשביל מה שבקרון המטען. בית אוריין העבירו נשקים עתיקים ברחבי חורוויר תחת מסווה של אחדות. אנחנו כאן כדי לעצור את זה. הישארו למטה. הישארו בשקט. וכשזה ייגמר, ספרו לילדים שלכם שראיתם משהו ששווה לזכור."*

## What [[Captain Ash|Ash]] Actually Wants
He wants [[The Statue]]. He believes it contains something dangerous that [[House Orien]] plans to weaponize. He is half-right. He does not know about [[The Siberys Shard]] — only that [[The Statue]] is important.

## Player Choices
- **Fight:** [[The Raiders]] are CR-appropriate but well-coordinated. [[Captain Ash|Ash]] will try to avoid killing.
- **Hide:** Plenty of cover in the dining car. [[Captain Ash|Ash]]'s crew will ignore passive players.
- **Negotiate:** [[Captain Ash|Ash]] is willing to talk. He respects soldiers. He distrusts the Houses.
- **Flee:** Moving between cars is dangerous but possible.

## Complications
- **Lady [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]]** is not in the dining car — she retreated to her private cabin when the lights flickered. Suspicious timing.
- **Marshal [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]]** and his guards engage [[The Raiders]] in the security car. Gunfire echoes.
- **[[Professor Nymm]]** is trying to get *to* the cargo car, not away from it. "I need to see it!"

## Transition to Scene 5
[[Captain Ash|Ash]]'s crew reports that the cargo car is secured but the manifest crate is locked with a complex arcane seal. [[Captain Ash|Ash]] looks at the players.

> *"You lot — you're veterans, aren't you? I can smell the war on you. Help me open that seal, and I'll make sure no one else dies tonight. Or stand in my way, and become another casualty of House greed."*

**Hebrew:**
> *"אתם — אתם חיילים משוחררים, נכון? אני מריח את המלחמה עליכם. עזרו לי לפתוח את החותם ההוא, ואני אדאג שאף אחד אחר לא ימות הלילה. או שעמדו בדרכי, והפכו לקורבן נוסף של חמדנות בית."*
