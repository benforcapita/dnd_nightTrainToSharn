# Scene 6 — [[The Statue]] Car

## Scene Purpose
- The climax of Session 1
- Reveal [[The Siberys Shard]]
- Force the players to make an irreversible choice
- End the session with a secret that binds them together

## The Cargo Car
A long, cold car filled with crates, boxes, and one massive object under a dusty tarp.
The air here is wrong. Colder than it should be. Thick. Heavy. Like breathing at the bottom of a deep lake.
Arcane lanterns flicker blue instead of warm yellow.

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **קרון המטען:** קרון ארוך וקפוא מלא בארגזים, קופסאות, ופריט אחד ענק תחת כיסוי מבולבל.
> האוויר כאן לא בסדר. קר יותר ממה שצריך. עבה. כבד. כמו לנשום בתחתית אגם עמוק.
> מנורות ארקאניות מהבהבות כחול במקום צהוב חם.

## [[The Statue]] Revealed
When the tarp is pulled back — by [[Captain Ash|Ash]], by the players, by accident — they see:

A **[[The Statue|Dhakaani]] stone sentinel**, eight feet tall, carved from black basalt. Its face is serene, almost smiling. Its hands are pressed together in a gesture of prayer — or binding.
The craftsmanship is impossibly precise. Every scale of its armor, every fold of its robes, every eyelash.
And across its chest: a crack. A hairline fracture that was not there when it was loaded.

**Read Aloud:**
> *"[[The Statue]] is ancient. Older than Galifar. Older than the Five Nations.
> Its eyes are closed, but you swear — you *swear* — it is watching you.
> The crack in its chest pulses with faint golden light. Not fire. Not lightning.
> Something older. Something that remembers.
> And when you touch it — even through gloves — your scars ache. Your breath catches.
> Your soul recognizes it, even if your mind does not."*

**Hebrew:**
> *"הפסל הוא עתיק. יותר מגליפאר. יותר מחמש האומות.
> עיניו עצומות, אך אתם נשבעים — אתם *נשבעים* — שהוא צופה בכם.
> הסדק בחזהו פועם באור זהב עמום. לא אש. לא ברק.
> משהו עתיק יותר. משהו שזוכר.
> וכשאתם נוגעים בו — אפילו דרך כפפות — הצלקות שלכם כואבות. הנשימה שלכם נתפסת.
> הנשמה שלכם מכירה אותו, גם אם השכל שלכם לא."*

## The Shard
Inside the crack — visible now as the arcane seal fails — rests a fragment of **Siberys dragonshard**.
- Roughly the size of a fist
- Translucent gold with internal swirling patterns like trapped storms
- Warm to the touch, almost alive
- Hums at a frequency felt in the sternum, not heard
- When exposed, the air tastes of ozone and copper

**If identified (Arcana DC 15 or [[Professor Nymm|Nymm]|Nymm]]'s help):**
This is a **[[The Siberys shard|Siberys shard]** — a fragment of the dragon above. They are incredibly rare, incredibly valuable, and incredibly dangerous. The dragonmarked houses would kill for one. The dragons of Argonnessen would start wars for one.

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **השבר:** בתוך הסדק — נראה כעת כשהחותם הארקאני נכשל — נח שבר של **דרקון סיביריס**.
> בערך בגודל אגרוף. זהוב שקוף עם תבניות מסתחררות פנימיות כמו סופות לכודות. חמים למגע, כמעט חי. מהמים בתדר שנרגם בחזה, לא נשמע.
> **אם מזוהה (Arcana DC 15 או עזרת נים):** זה שבר סיביריס — שבר של הדרקון מלמעלה. הם נדירים בקצבה, בעלי ערך עצום, ומסוכנים בקצבה. בתי הדרקון היו הורגים בשביל אחד. הדרקונים של ארגונסן היו מתחילים מלחמות בשביל אחד.

## The Choice
Everyone in the cargo car wants the shard. Here's what each party wants:

| Party | Goal | What They Offer |
|-------|------|---------------|
| **[[Captain Ash]]** | Take the shard away from House control | Truth, protection, a cause |
| **Marshal [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]]** | Secure it for [[House Orien]] / Deneith | Safety, gold, House patronage |
| **[[Professor Nymm]]** | Study it for "the greater good" | Knowledge, academic credibility |
| **Lady [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]]** (if present) | Ensure it reaches [[Sharn]] as planned | Political power, advancement |

**[[The Raiders]] are not done.** As the party debates, reinforcements arrive — a second wave, or the first wave regroups. The train is still under attack.

## The Players' Secret
In the chaos — a final explosion, a lurch, bodies thrown — the players are the only ones left conscious in the cargo car.
The shard is in their hands. Or under their coat. Or in a pocket.
They have seconds before the doors open and someone sees them.

**DM:** *"What do you do?"*

## Transition to Ending
The train lurches toward [[Sharn]]. The fighting dies down — either because [[Captain Ash|Ash]] retreated, was captured, or was killed. But the players have something now. Something heavy. Something that hums.
And when they step off the train at [[Terminus Station]], no one will know.
No one but them.
