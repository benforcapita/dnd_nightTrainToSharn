# Session 1 — Loot and Consequences

## Loot

### Guaranteed
- **[[The Siberys shard|Siberys shard]|Siberys Dragonshard Fragment]]** — see [[The Siberys Shard]]
  - Roughly fist-sized, warm, humming
  - Worth more than a kingdom; impossible to sell openly
  - May have properties the players do not yet understand

### Optional / Conditional
- **Raider Equipment** — if players loot bodies (moral ambiguity!)
  - 2x [[House Orien]] uniforms (for disguises?)
  - 1x Raider Arcane Goggles (low-light vision, detect magic 1/day)
  - Various weapons and tools
- **Deneith Guard Gear** — if players help [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]]
  - Deneith identification papers (limited access)
  - 50 gp "gratitude payment" (hush money)
- **[[Professor Nymm]]'s Notes** — if players stopped him from fleeing
  - Fragmented research on [[The Statue|Dhakaani]] binding rituals
  - A sketch of [[The Statue]] with annotations in Draconic
  - A letter addressed to "[[The Aurum]] — [[Sharn]] Chapter"
- **Lady [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]]'s Gratitude** — if players protected her
  - An emerald-inked business card: "For services rendered. — E.O."
  - Invitation to a private reception in [[Sharn]] (Upper Central)
- **Veteran's Silver Ring** — from the drunk soldier at the bar
  - Unit insignia from the [[Timeline|Last War]] — possible plot hook later
  - Worth 5 sp sentimentally; priceless narratively

## Consequences

### Immediate (Session 2)
- [[House Orien]] will conduct internal investigations
- [[House Deneith]] will file reports; [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]] may remember the players
- [[The Raiders]] may contact the players if [[Captain Ash|Ash]] survived
- [[Professor Nymm]] has vanished — and he knows the players have the shard
- Word spreads in [[Sharn]]'s underworld that "something valuable arrived on [[The Firefly]]"

### Short-Term (Sessions 3-6)
- Criminal syndicates begin sniffing around
- Dragonmarked houses offer "employment" (cover for surveillance)
- [[Sharn|Morgrave University]] scholars approach with questions
- A mysterious patron offers to buy "any artifacts from [[The Firefly]] incident"
- The players' lodgings are searched while they're out

### Long-Term (Campaign Arcs)
- See [[Future Campaign Arcs]]
- The shard's true nature drives the entire campaign
- Every faction in [[Sharn]] will eventually learn the players have it — or think they do
