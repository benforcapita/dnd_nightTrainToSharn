# Scene 1 — Terminus Station (Dawn)

### Atmosphere
The train pulls in. Battered. Smoke-stained. House Orien staff swarm the platform. Nobles are whisked away. The Sharn Inquisitive presses for statements. No one looks at the veterans.

### Read Aloud
> *"Terminus Station swallows the Firefly like a whale accepting a wounded calf. The platform is chaos — officials shouting into sending stones, Deneith guards forming corridors for the wealthy, porters hauling luggage while pretending not to see the bloodstains.
> 
> No one meets you. No one asks your name. You are four more veterans in a city of millions.
> 
> Above you, Sharn rises. And rises. And rises. Towers disappear into clouds. Bridges thread between them like spider silk. Skycoaches drift like lazy fish. Somewhere up there, people who matter are making decisions that will change your lives.
> 
> Right now, you just need a place to sleep."*

### Key Details
- The party slips away from the station unnoticed — or nearly. A journalist asks one question too many. A Deneith guard watches them too long.
- **Marshal Serik ir'Deneith** may acknowledge them — a nod, a warning, or an offer of "assistance" (surveillance)
- **Professor Nymm** is nowhere to be seen. He vanished during the chaos. The party may wonder where he went.
- The shard hums faintly as they step onto Sharn's stones — the city *reacts* to it somehow, or it reacts to the city

### The Shard's First Sharn Reaction
When the party first steps onto Sharn's streets, the shard pulses. Characters holding it feel a moment of vertigo — not physical, but *temporal*. A flash of something: a workshop, goblin hands, blue fire, a word in a language they don't speak.

**Arcana DC 12:** The shard is resonating with something beneath the city.
**No check:** the shard just said *hello*. Or *finally*.
