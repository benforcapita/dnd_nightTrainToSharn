# Scene 2 — The Streets of Sharn

### Atmosphere
The party must navigate from Terminus (Lower Tavick's Landing) to wherever they're staying. This is their first taste of Sharn's vertical madness.

### Encounter: The City Is Alive
Roll or choose 2-3:
- A skycoach crashes into a tower three bridges away — debris rains down
- Boromar halfling on a clawfoot dinosaur chases a thief through the crowd
- Lord of Blades — passersby ignore him
- House Cannith demonstration: a mechanical dragonfly flits through the crowd, recording faces
- A shifter pickpocket targets the party; if caught, they offer information instead of the wallet
- A newspaper boy shouts the headline: "FIREFLY SABOTAGE — ORIEN DENIES TERROR LINK"

### Read Aloud (City Transition)
> *"Sharn doesn't have streets. It has layers. You walk on bridges that cross between towers, climb staircases that spiral through them, ride lifts that groan as they haul you upward. Every level has its own light — the wealthy bask in filtered sun; the poor navigate by everbright lanterns that never quite reach the corners.
> 
> The city smells of rain, industry, and something arcane — ozone from the conductor stones, incense from a thousand temples, the sharp tang of dragonshard dust. People press past you in a dozen species, a hundred languages. Half of them are armed. Half of them are lying.
> 
> You are not in the war anymore. But you are not safe."*
