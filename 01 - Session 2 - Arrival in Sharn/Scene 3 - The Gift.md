# Scene 3 — The Gift

### Setup
Sometime during their first 24 hours in Sharn, the party receives a message. The source depends on Session 1 choices:

| If... | Then... |
|---|---|
| They helped Lady Elira | She sends a courier with the deed, a letter of gratitude, and an invitation to Skyway |
| They helped Marshal Serik | Deneith arranges a "settlement" — the deed and a warning that House Orien|House Orien considers the matter closed |
| They saved civilians | A grateful passenger (minor noble, merchant, or veteran) gifts them the deed — it's all they had to give |
| None of the above | A House Orien|House Orien legal fixer shows up. "Sign this. Take the deed. The Firefly|The Firefly never happened. We never met." |

### The Deed
A weathered parchment granting ownership of **The The Dragons Rest|Dragon's Rest** — Bazaar and Sharn|Firelight wards. The previous owner: a Cyran refugee who stopped paying taxes three years ago and was recently declared legally deceased.

**The Catch:** The deed comes with back taxes (100 gp), a failed health inspection (the kitchen is a crime scene), and a rival (Emmek d'Ghallanda). The party can pay the taxes later, but the city expects them within 30 days.
