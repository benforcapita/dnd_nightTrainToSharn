# Scene 7 — The First Night

### Setup
The party's first night in their new home. They're exhausted. The shard hums. Sharn's lights glitter through dirty windows. For the first time since the train, they can rest.

### The Shard's Dream
One character (the shard, or the one with the highest passive Wisdom) has a dream:

> *"You stand in a workshop that predates human civilization. Goblin artificers — goblins, but taller, prouder, marked with ritual scars — work at forges that burn blue. They sing as they work, a marching song in a language that sounds like stone grinding stone.
> 
> At the center of the workshop, a floating matrix of light and runes — the schema. Blueprints that exist in three dimensions, rotating slowly, updating as the artificers chant.
> 
> One goblin turns. Looks directly at you — through centuries, through stone, through the shard in your pocket.
> 
> 'You are late,' she says. 'The door has been waiting.'
> 
> You wake with the taste of copper in your mouth and the shard warm in your hand."*
