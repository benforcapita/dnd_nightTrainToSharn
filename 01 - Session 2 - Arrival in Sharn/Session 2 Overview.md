# Session 2 — Arrival in Sharn

## Session Goal
Transition from the claustrophobic train to the overwhelming vertical city. Give the party their new home and let them *learn* it — not just the pub, but the bridge it sits on, the neighbors around it, the history buried in its stones. By the end, the weight of what they need to pay should land like a hammer.

## Pacing
Slow, atmospheric, exploratory. This session is about discovery — the city, the bridge, the people. Two-thirds exploration and relationship-building, one-third "oh gods we're broke." The money bomb should hit late enough that the party has already fallen in love with the place.
