# Scene 1 — Rebuilding

### Setup
A week passes. The party works on the pub — cleaning, repairing, hiring. This is a montage opportunity.

### Renovation Progress
Track what the party invests (time and gold):

| Investment | Result |
|---|---|
| Clean the taproom (1 day) | Functional common room; attracts first customers |
| Fix the kitchen (2 days, 100 gp) | Can serve food; halfling health inspector passes |
| Restore the rooms (3 days, 200 gp) | Can rent to travelers; 2d6 gp/week income |
| Fix the bells (1 week, 50 gp) | Alarm system; The Dragons Rest|Torvin becomes helpful spirit |
| Full renovation (2 weeks, 1,000 gp) | The pub is beautiful; reputation, income, and faction attention all increase |

### Hiring Staff
The party can hire staff. Every hire is an opportunity for infiltration:

| Role | Suggested NPC | Possible Connection |
|---|---|---|
| **Bartender** | Kaela, a Cyran veteran who lost her family in the Mourning | Genuine. No faction ties. Fiercely loyal if treated well. |
| **Cook** | Three-Tooth, a goblin with a talent for dwarven cuisine | Former Daask. Left the gang. They might want him back. |
| **Bouncer** | Jorun, a warforged with a broken voicebox and a strong right arm | Neutral. But warforged attract the Future Villains|Lord of Blades' attention. |
| **Server** | Mira, a changeling who "forgets" to mention they're a changeling | Tyrant operative. Or just a changeling. The party can't know which. |
| **Cleaner** | Old Marta, grandmotherly human who knows *everyone* | She's nobody's spy. But she gossips. Every faction learns what she learns. |

### The Guilds Arrive
Sharn's guilds demand attention:
- **Bakers' and Brewers' Guild:** 25 gp license fee. Emmek has already filed a complaint about "substandard facilities."
- **Innkeepers' Association:** 50 gp membership. Provides customers and legal protection. Requires inspection.
- **Bridge Maintenance Collective:** The bridge needs work. 10 gp/month or they "can't guarantee structural integrity." (Boromar Clan|Boromar Clan|Boromar Clan|Boromar Clan.)
