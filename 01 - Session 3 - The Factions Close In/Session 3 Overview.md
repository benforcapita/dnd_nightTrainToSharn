# Session 3 — The Factions Close In

## Session Goal
The party needs gold to save the Dragon's Rest — and they need it together. This session is about the party working as a team to earn serious money, while discovering that every helping hand in Sharn comes with strings attached. Factions approach openly and covertly throughout the week. By the end, the pub is saved (or close to it), but the party has made at least one ally and at least one enemy — and someone makes a move against them.

## Pacing
The first half is action-oriented: questing across Sharn, earning gold, discovering the city ward by ward. The second half is social: faction emissaries parade through, each with an offer that's equal parts help and hook. The session ends with an action beat — a threat to the pub that proves the stakes are real.

## The Money Arc
This session should feel like the party *solving* the problem Session 2 dropped on them. They know what they need (~175 gp minimum). They go out and earn it. But every quest they take plants a faction seed, every contact they make adds a layer of complication, and by the time they've got the gold, they've also got attention they can't shake.

## Scene Sequence

1. **Scene 1 — Rebuilding** (the morning after: planning, hiring, first renovations)
2. **Scene 2 — Earning Your Keep** (the questing week — 2-3 jobs across Sharn's wards)
3. **Scene 3 — The Faction Parade** (emissaries throughout the week — social encounters)
4. **Scene 4 — The Threat** (someone makes a move — the session's action beat)
5. **Scene 5 — The Party Decides** (who to trust, what to prioritize, how to move forward)
