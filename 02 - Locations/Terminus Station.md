# Terminus Station

## Overview
The lightning rail terminus in [[Sharn]] — a cathedral of glass, brass, and controlled chaos.
Trains arrive from across [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]]. Passengers flood onto platforms. [[House Orien]] officials process manifests with practiced efficiency.
Above, the towers of [[Sharn]] loom like jealous gods.
Below, [[Sharn|The Cogs]] breathe heat and ancient dust.

## The Morning After
When [[The Firefly]] arrives, Terminus Station is a hive of activity:
- [[House Orien]] officials whisk VIPs away before journalists arrive
- Inquisitive reporters swarm survivors looking for quotes
- Deneith guards cordon off the platform
- City Watch officers take statements no one will read
- Pickpockets and information brokers circle like vultures

## Key NPCs Present
- **Inspector Varn** ([[Sharn]] City Watch) — tired, perceptive, asks too many questions
- **Kela the Scribe** — a chronicler who buys stories from survivors; pays in gold and introductions
- **"Fingers" Joss** — a halfling information broker; knows everything worth knowing

## DM Notes
- This is the players' first taste of [[Sharn]]
- They arrive as anonymous survivors of a "terrorist attack"
- No one suspects them — yet
- Use this scene to introduce the city's scale, its factions, and its hunger for stories
