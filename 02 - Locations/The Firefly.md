# The Firefly

## Overview
The Firefly is [[House Orien]]'s newest lightning rail prototype — faster, sleeker, and more luxurious than anything that has come before.
It was built to symbolize unity after the [[Timeline|Last War]]. In reality, it symbolizes [[House Orien]]'s ambition to replace national identity with corporate loyalty.

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **גחלילית** היא אב-הטיפוס החדש ביותר של בית אוריין לרכבת הברקים — מהירה, חלקה, ומפוארת יותר מכל מה שהיה לפניה.
> נבנתה כסמל לאחדות לאחר המלחמה האחרונה. במציאות, היא מסמלת את שאפתנות בית אוריין להחליף זהות לאומית בנאמנות תאגידית.

## Features
- **Speed:** 25% faster than standard lightning rails
- **Luxury:** First-class amenities for propaganda purposes
- **Elemental Binding:** Experimental conductor stones — potentially unstable
- **Security:** [[House Deneith]] contract; biometric arcane locks
- **Symbolism:** Every car is designed to showcase "progress"

## Train Cars

### 1. Engine Car
- Off-limits to passengers
- Contains the bound elemental spirit that drives the train
- The spirit is... louder than usual. Restless.

### 2. Conductor Stone Array
- The arcane engine room
- Blue-white energy arcs between crystalline matrices
- Unsafe to enter without protective gear

### 3. Security Car
- [[House Deneith]] guard quarters
- Weapon racks, communication crystals, monitoring station
- Heavily warded against magical intrusion

### 4. Cargo Car
- Where [[The Statue]] is hidden
- Arcane climate control (unnecessarily cold)
- Double-sealed locks; House Cannith oversight manifest
- **The heart of the mystery**

### 5. Crew Quarters
- Attendants, engineers, cooks
- cramped but clean
- One attendant has been taking notes about the cargo

### 6. Dining Car
- Seats 40 in silver-service luxury
- Galifar-era china and crystal
- The players' "honored guest" table

### 7. First Class Lounge
- Leather sofas, reading library, string quartet
- Lady [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira]]'s unofficial throne room
- A fire elemental in the hearth (controlled, expensive)

### 8. Passenger Cars
- Standard accommodations
- Most passengers are minor nobles, journalists, House functionaries
- One car is almost entirely empty — "reserved for dignitaries who canceled"

### 9. Observation Car
- Glass walls, panoramic views
- The safest place to breathe
- Also the most exposed if the train is attacked from above

## Atmosphere
Warm brass lighting. Polished wood interiors. Arcane crystal lamps.
Soft orchestral music. Constant humming from the conductor stones.
The train feels expensive, modern, and slightly unsettling.
Like a beautiful coffin.

## Battle Map Notes
- Cars are 30ft x 15ft each
- Connecting sections are 10ft platforms with waist-high railings
- Moving between cars during combat requires **DC 12 Acrobatics** or risk falling
- The roof is flat but windy — **DC 14 Strength (Athletics)** to stay upright in high speed
