# Captain Ash

## Overview
Leader of the raider attack on [[The Firefly]]. Former veteran of the [[Timeline|Last War]].
Professional, intelligent, and deeply distrustful of the dragonmarked houses.
Ash is not cruel for the sake of cruelty. He believes the Houses are becoming too powerful, that dangerous artifacts are being hidden, and that another war is inevitable.
His motivation: steal the hidden [[The Siberys shard|Siberys shard] before it disappears into House-controlled territory.
**Important:** Ash does NOT know exactly what the shard does. Only that multiple powerful factions are searching for it.

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **קפטן אש** — מנהל ההתקפה של השודדים על גחלילית. חייל משוחרר לשעבר מהמלחמה האחרונה. מקצועי, חכם, וחשוד עמוקות כלפי בתי הדרקון. אש לא אכזרי לשם אכזריות. הוא מאמין שהבתים הופכים חזקים מדי, שארטיפקטים מסוכנים מוטמנים, ושמלחמה נוספת בלתי נמנעת.

## Appearance
- Late 30s, weathered face with a scar across the left eyebrow
- Wears a longcoat reinforced with arcane weave — functional, not flashy
- Carries two shortswords and a hand crossbow — standard military issue, well-maintained
- Grey-streaked hair pulled back in a practical tail
- Eyes that have seen too much and still look for the next threat

## Personality
- Speaks plainly, no flourish. Every word costs something.
- Respects competence and courage; has no patience for bureaucracy or inherited privilege
- Deeply protective of his crew — they are the only family he has left
- Not a revolutionary ideologue; a pragmatist who ran out of legal options

## Relationships
- **His Crew:** Family. He has buried six of them. He will not bury more.
- **[[House Deneith]] / [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik]]:** Bitter enemies on paper. But they fought on opposite sides of the same battle once. Neither has forgotten.
- **The Players:** He sees himself in them — broken, used, discarded. He wants them to wake up.
- **Whoever Tipped Him:** He doesn't know their identity. He received coded messages through a dead drop in [[Sharn|The Cogs]].

## Secrets
- Ash is dying. A wasting disease from a chemical weapon exposure during the war. He has maybe a year.
- He has a daughter in [[Sharn]] — lower wards — whom he has not seen in five years. He does not know she is alive.
- His real name is not Ash. It was something else, from somewhere else, before the war burned it away.
- He has been contacted by [[The Aurum]] but refused to work with them — "I steal from thieves, I don't join them."

## Hebrew Name Note
Ash speaks Hebrew with a rough, working-class accent. He uses slang and short forms. His Hebrew name would be "אפר" (Efer) but he never uses it.
