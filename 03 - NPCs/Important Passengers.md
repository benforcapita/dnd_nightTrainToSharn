# Important Passengers

## Overview
Beyond the main NPCs, [[The Firefly]] carries a cross-section of post-war [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]] — each with their own agendas, prejudices, and secrets.
These passengers can be allies, complications, or red herrings.

---

## Duchess Velara ir'Wynarn
- **Identity:** Distant cousin to the Brelish royal line; minor nobility with major debts
- **On the train:** Fleeing creditors in [[Thronehold]]; hoping to disappear into [[Sharn]]'s lower wards
- **Personality:** Haughty, terrified, drinks too much wine
- **Secret:** She sold information about [[The Firefly|Firefly]] cargo to [[The Aurum]] for 500 gp
- **Hebrew Voice:** Formal, archaic, uses "אני" like a royal decree

**Quote:**
> *"I don't care what they're smuggling. I care that my cabin has a lock. Do you know what it's like to be hunted by your own creditors? No. Of course not. You people have the luxury of simplicity."*

**Hebrew:**
> *"לא אכפת לי מה הם מבריחים. אכפת לי שלביתי שלי יש מנעול. אתם יודעים איך זה להיות נרדף על ידי הנושים שלך? לא. כמובן שלא. לאנשים כמוכם יש את היוקרה של הפשטות."*

---

## Brother Aldric (Thranish Priest)
- **Identity:** A priest of the Silver Flame assigned to "spiritual oversight" of veterans
- **On the train:** Officially pastoral care; unofficially reporting to Thrane intelligence
- **Personality:** Kind eyes, judgmental tongue, genuinely believes he is helping
- **Secret:** He has been ordered to identify veterans who might be "sympathetic to Thrane restoration"
- **Hebrew Voice:** Preachy, uses biblical Hebrew constructions

**Quote:**
> *"The Flame purifies, my child. But sometimes purification looks like fire. The war burned away our illusions. What rises from the [[Captain Ash|Ash]] is stronger. What refuses to burn... must be kindled."*

**Hebrew:**
> *"הלהב מטהר, ילדי. אך לפעמים טיהור נראה כמו אש. המלחמה שרפה את האשליות שלנו. מה שעולה מהאפר חזק יותר. מה שמסרב להישרף... חייב להיות מודלק."*

---

## Jax "The Wrench" Torval
- **Identity:** A warforged artificer; former battlefield medic; now House Cannith consultant
- **On the train:** Inspecting the experimental conductor stones; officially quality assurance
- **Personality:** Dry humor, unexpectedly gentle, obsessed with mechanical perfection
- **Secret:** The conductor stones are unstable. He reported this. He was told to be quiet.
- **Hebrew Voice:** Literal, precise, occasional dry jokes that land perfectly

**Quote:**
> *"I have calculated the probability of this train exploding. It is higher than Lady [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]] would prefer. I told her. She smiled. I do not understand human expressions, but I believe that was the wrong one."*

**Hebrew:**
> *"חישבתי את הסתברות הפיצוץ של רכבת זו. היא גבוהה יותר ממה שליידי אלירה הייתה מעדיפה. אמרתי לה. היא חייכה. אני לא מבין הבעות אנושיות, אך אני מאמין שזו הייתה ההבעה הלא נכונה."*

---

## Mira d'Sivis
- **Identity:** A young dragonmarked heir of House Sivis — messenger service and translation
- **On the train:** Delivering sealed diplomatic pouches; her first solo assignment
- **Personality:** Eager, nervous, trying too hard to be professional
- **Secret:** One of her pouches contains a message about [[The Statue]] — addressed to someone in [[Sharn]]'s City Watch
- **Hebrew Voice:** Fast, professional, occasionally slips into youth slang

**Quote:**
> *"I don't read the messages, obviously. That would be unethical. But the seals on this one are... different. House Cannith. [[House Orien]]. And something I don't recognize. A private mark? I'm probably imagining things. I imagine things when I'm nervous."*

**Hebrew:**
> *"אני לא קוראת את ההודעות, כמובן. זה יהיה לא אתי. אך החותמות על זו... שונות. בית קאנית. בית אוריין. ומשהו שאני לא מכירה. סימן פרטי? אני כנראה מדמיינת דברים. אני מדמיינת כשאני לחוצה."*

---

## Sergeant Borin "One-Eye" Kess
- **Identity:** A Cyran veteran; lost his unit during the [[Timeline|Mourning]]; now a beggar and informant
- **On the train:** Stowed away in the cargo car; discovered during the chaos of the raid
- **Personality:** Bitter, loyal to a dead country, surprisingly funny
- **Secret:** He saw the [[Timeline|Mourning]] from the edge. He knows things about it that no one believes.
- **Hebrew Voice:** Gravelly, sarcastic, uses outdated Cyran slang

**Quote:**> *"Cyre's gone. I'm not. That's the joke, isn't it? The whole nation turns to mist and I'm still here, stealing bread and telling stories no one believes. You want to know what I saw? I saw the sky turn inside out. That's what I saw. Now buy me a drink."*

**Hebrew:**
> *"סייר נעלמה. אני לא. זו הבדיחה, נכון? האומה כולה הופכת לערפל ואני עדיין כאן, גונב לחם ומספר סיפורים שאף אחד לא מאמין. רוצים לדעת מה ראיתי? ראיתי את השמיים מתהפכים. זה מה שראיתי. עכשיו קנו לי שתייה."*
