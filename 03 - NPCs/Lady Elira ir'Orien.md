# Lady Elira ir'Orien

## Overview
A rising star in [[House Orien]] — young, beautiful, politically savvy, and absolutely ruthless beneath a mask of progressive idealism.
Elira organized [[The Firefly]] maiden voyage and the "veterans' honor program" as a publicity stunt. But she also authorized the transport of the [[The Statue|Dhakaani]] statue. She knows more than she admits.
She does not know about [[The Siberys Shard]]. That was above her clearance.

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **ליידי אלירה איר'אוריין** — כוכבת עולה בבית אוריין — צעירה, יפה, חכמת פוליטיקה, ואכזרית לחלוטין מתחת למסכת אידיאליזם פרוגרסיבי.

## Appearance
- Early 30s, chestnut hair in elaborate Galifar-era braids
- Emerald [[House Orien]] silks that cost more than a soldier's yearly wage
- A lightning-rail brooch at her throat — the new symbol of [[House Orien]]
- Perfect posture from years of training, not breeding
- Eyes the color of storm clouds over the Mournland

## Personality
- Speaks like she's addressing a crowd even in private
- Remembers names, debts, and slights with equal precision
- Genuinely believes she is doing what is best for [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]] — she just defines "best" as "[[House Orien]]'s interests"
- Cannot admit weakness; will escalate rather than apologize

## Relationships
- **[[Professor Nymm]]:** Useful academic credibility. She finds him disgusting.
- **[[House Orien]] Elders:** She is proving herself. This train is her test. Failure is not an option.
- **The Players:** Useful props in her photo opportunity. Until they aren't.

## Secrets
- She authorized [[The Statue]] transport under pressure from [[The Twelve]]
- She does not know what is inside [[The Statue]]
- She has been receiving anonymous threats about [[The Firefly]] for weeks
- She carries a sending stone linked directly to the Orien patriarch

## Combat Notes
- Elira is not a fighter. She is a bard (or noble-equivalent) who uses words and influence as weapons.
- If combat breaks out, she retreats to her cabin and activates protective wards.
- She will negotiate with anyone — including [[The Raiders|Raiders]] — if it preserves her political position.
- CR: 1/4 (non-combatant with high AC from wards and connections)

## Hebrew Name Note
In Hebrew dialogue, she refers to herself in formal feminine: "אני מאמינה" (ani ma'amina). Her speech is polished, almost poetic.
