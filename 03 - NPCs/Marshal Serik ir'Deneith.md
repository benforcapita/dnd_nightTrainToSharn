# Marshal Serik ir'Deneith

## Overview
A veteran soldier of the [[Timeline|Last War]] who sold his sword to [[House Deneith]] after the Treaty.
Serik is not cruel, but he is cold. He believes in order, contracts, and the chain of command. The world is a machine; his job is to keep it running smoothly.
He has killed more people than he can remember. He does not sleep well.

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **מרשל סריק איר'דינית** — חייל משוחרר מהמלחמה האחרונה שמכר את חרבו לבית דינית לאחר ההסכם. סריק לא אכזרי, אך הוא קר. הוא מאמין בסדר, בחוזים, ובשרשרת הפיקוד.

## Appearance
- Late 40s, shaved head scarred from a Thrane flamethrower
- [[House Deneith]] armor — functional, polished, conspicuously worn
- A sword that has been reforged three times; he names it "Treaty"
- Dark circles under pale grey eyes
- Moves like a man who has memorized every angle of every room

## Personality
- Speaks in short, declarative sentences
- Does not ask questions he doesn't already know the answer to
- Treats every social interaction like a military briefing
- Has a dry, unexpected sense of humor that only surfaces after he trusts someone

## Relationships
- **Lady [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]]:** "The client. Nothing more."
- **[[Captain Ash]]:** He knows [[Captain Ash|Ash]] from the war — different units, same battlefields. He will not admit this.
- **The Players:** Potential assets or liabilities. Nothing more. Nothing less.

## Secrets
- He has been keeping a journal of [[House Orien]]'s "unusual transports" for three months
- He suspects [[The Twelve]] are involved but has no proof
- He carries a sealed letter to be opened only upon his death — addressed to a Cyran orphanage
- He has killed [[House Orien]] personnel before, on Deneith orders. He remembers their faces.
