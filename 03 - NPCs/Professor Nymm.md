# Professor Nymm

## Overview
A gnome scholar attached to [[Sharn|Morgrave University]] — officially studying "post-war transportation infrastructure," unofficially obsessed with [[The Statue|Dhakaani]] artifacts and the [[Prophecy Fragments|Draconic Prophecy]].
Nymm is on [[The Firefly]] because he brokered access to [[The Statue]]. He believes it is a key to understanding the [[Timeline|Mourning]]. He is not wrong. He is also not safe.

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **פרופסור נים** — חוקר גמד מצורף לאוניברסיטת מורגייב — רשמית חוקר "תשתיות תחבורה שלאחר מלחמה", לא רשמית אובססיבי לארטיפקטים דהאקאניים ולנבואה הדרקונית.

## Appearance
- Ancient even by gnome standards — perhaps 350 years old
- Coats layered like archaeological strata — each one a different decade's fashion
- Spectacles with lenses that shift color based on what he's looking at (enchanted)
- Ink-stained fingers that twitch when he's excited
- Smells of old vellum, cinnamon, and something faintly electrical

## Personality
- Speaks in rapid, tangential lectures — answers questions with more questions
- Genuinely kind but socially oblivious
- Becomes completely focused when examining artifacts; forgets to eat, sleep, or flee
- Terrified of violence but fascinated by its history

## Relationships
- **Lady [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]]:** Suspicious of her motives but needs her access
- **Professor Nymm's "Collector":** Someone he calls "the Reader" has been feeding him information about [[House Orien]]'s artifact transports.

## Secrets
- Nymm has already seen a [[The Siberys shard|Siberys shard] once — during an expedition to Xen'drik. He never told anyone what it showed him.
- He knows [[The Statue]] contains something but does not know it's a shard. He thinks it's a "prophecy codex."
- He is being slowly poisoned by someone at [[Sharn|Morgrave]] — low doses of dreamlily extract in his tea. It is making him more paranoid.
- He has a sending stone connected to [[The Aurum]] but has never used it. He is afraid of what they want.
