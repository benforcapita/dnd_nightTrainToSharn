# House Deneith

## Overview
The House of the Sentinel. Mark of the Beast ( griffon / גריפון ). Mercenaries, bodyguards, and security contractors.
The blades you hire when you want clean hands and dirty work.

## Philosophy
"Contracts are sacred."
They do not ask why. They ask how much and for how long.
But since the Treaty, some Deneith veterans are starting to ask why anyway.

## Role in the Campaign
- Providing security for [[The Firefly]]
- [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]] and his guards are embedded operatives
- Will hunt the players if [[House Orien]] orders it
- May offer "protection" that is actually surveillance

## Key Members
- **[[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]] ir'Deneith]]** — honorable man in a dishonorable job
- **Commander Aldra d'Deneith** — [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]]'s superior; cold, efficient, dangerous
- **The Bladebreakers** — an elite unit for "sensitive" operations

> **בית דינית** — בית השומר. סימן החיה (גריפון). שכירי חרב, מאבטחים, וקבלני ביטחון. הלהבות שאתה שוכר כשאתה רוצה ידיים נקיות ועבודה מלוכלכת.
