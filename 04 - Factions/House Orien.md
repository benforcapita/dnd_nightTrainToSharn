# House Orien

## Overview
The House of [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Passage]]. Mark of the Beast ( unicorn / ראם ). Masters of transportation, communication, and logistics across [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]].
If House Orien wanted to, they could stop the continent. Every train, every courier, every message — they touch it all.

## Philosophy
"We connect what was broken."
Officially: rebuilding unity after the [[Timeline|Last War]].
Actually: replacing national infrastructure with corporate dependency.

## Role in the Campaign
- Created [[The Firefly]] as propaganda
- Transporting [[The Statue]] "as a favor" to [[The Twelve]]
- Will deny everything if exposed
- Sees the players as either assets or threats

## Key Members
- **[[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]] ir'Orien]]** — ambitious, rising star
- **Patriarch Yoren d'Orien** — old, cautious, suspects something went wrong
- **Quartermaster Vex** — knows where everything is; dangerous if bribed
