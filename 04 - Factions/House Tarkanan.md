# House Tarkanan

## Overview
Not a dragonmarked house — an anti-house. House Tarkanan is a secret society of aberrant dragonmarked individuals: people whose dragonmarks are corrupted, dangerous, and feared by the "pure" houses. Named after Halas Tarkanan, who destroyed Old Sharn during the War of the Mark, they are terrorists, freedom fighters, and victims — depending on who you ask.

They believe the dragonmarked houses have committed genocide against aberrant-marked people for centuries. And they're not wrong.

## Philosophy
"Your mark is pure. Ours is powerful. They called us abominations. We call ourselves the future."

House Tarkanan doesn't want reform. They want destruction. Every dragonmarked house that participated in the purges must fall. Every heir of a pure mark is a descendant of murderers. They are not asking for an apology. They are collecting a debt.

## Structure
- **Oleander Nightbloom** — drow aberrant-mark assassin and alchemist; bartender at Nightshade in Shae Lias. Master of toxins. The main villain if House Tarkanan is the primary antagonist
- **The Shrouds** — Tarkanan's elite operatives; each bears an aberrant dragonmark that grants combat abilities beyond normal magic
- **Buzz** — a warforged swashbuckler who fights with panache and genuinely believes in the cause; a romantic among terrorists
- **The Sympathizers** — warforged, shifters, changelings, and others who've been marginalized by the Houses

## Territory
- **Shae Lias (Upper Tavick's Landing)** — Nightshade tavern, Oleander's base of operations
- **The Depths** — hidden safehouses and training grounds
- **Abandoned Cannith facilities** — symbolic sites for Tarkanan operations
- **The Broken Mirror** — a Tarkanan hideout accessible through mirrored portals

## Role in the Campaign

### As the Main Villain
House Tarkanan wants the schema to build a weapon that neutralizes dragonmarks — all of them, pure and aberrant alike. Oleander Nightbloom's plan: use the Storm Spire to create an anti-mark field across Khorvaire. Without their marks, the Houses are just merchants. And House Tarkanan knows exactly where every house leader sleeps.

### As a Secondary Faction
They offer the party knowledge about aberrants, access to hidden safehouses, and a network of spies who hate the Houses as much as any war veteran might. But alliance with Tarkanan means embracing terrorism — the party must decide if the cause justifies the methods.

### Patron Approach
Oleander Nightbloom slides a cocktail across the bar at the Dragon's Rest that no one remembers him ordering. "The Houses used you in the war. They're using you now. When they're done, they'll discard you like every other veteran. We offer something different: a chance to burn it all down."

## Key Members
- **Oleander Nightbloom** — drow assassin, master alchemist and artificer; his cocktails are legendary, and so is his poison
- **Buzz** — warforged swashbuckler; believes in the cause with the zeal of a convert; genuinely caring, genuinely dangerous
- **The Shrouds** — cadre of aberrant-marked operatives; each Shroud has a unique aberrant power (teleportation, flesh-warping, death-touch)
- **The Tarkanan Historians** — scholars who document the purges and recruit from the traumatized

## House Tarkanan as Villain — Encounter Chain (Autumn)

The schema has passed through: Oleander Nightbloom → various dead drops → the Broken Mirror hideout → Gorgon's Run.

### Encounter Path
1. **The Crime Scene** — a Tarkanan operative was killed in the explosion; their aberrant mark is still visible on their corpse
2. **Nightshade Tavern (Shae Lias)** — Oleander runs the bar and the operation. A social encounter with a master poisoner.
3. **The Broken Mirror** — a Tarkanan hideout. Mirrors are portals. Not all of them lead where they should.
4. **Gorgon's Run** — an abandoned Cannith facility where Tarkanan is already testing the schema on captured House heirs
5. **The Confrontation** — Oleander and his Shrouds make their stand

### Weather: Cool Winds
Crisp autumn air. Leaves (imported from Aundair) line the upper wards. The wind carries whispers — Tarkanan uses weather as communication channels. Everbright lanterns flicker as aberrant magic interferes.

### Finale Location: Gorgon's Run
An abandoned Cannith facility beneath Sharn. Filled with broken creation forges, failed warforged experiments, and the ghosts of purged aberrant-mark bearers. Tarkanan has turned it into a temple to their cause — and a prison for captured House heirs.
