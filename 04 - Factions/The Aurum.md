# The Aurum

## Overview
A secret society of wealthy industrialists, crime lords, and exiled nobles.
They believe the dragonmarked houses have grown too powerful. Their solution: become more powerful themselves.
The Aurum does not fight wars. They buy the people who do.

## Philosophy
"Gold is neutral. Gold does not mourn. Gold decides."
They collect artifacts, information, and influence like other people collect stamps.

## Role in the Campaign
- Probably tipped off [[Captain Ash]] about [[The Statue]]
- Want the shard for leverage, not power
- Will approach the players with "generous offers"
- Cannot be trusted — but their information is always accurate

## Key Members
- **[[Future Villains|Lord Vex ir'Tharn]]** — [[Sharn]]'s Aurum mastermind
- **The Gilded Lady** — information broker; no one knows her real face
- **Coinkeeper Morrik** — accountant for the undead; runs the [[Sharn]] chapter's finances

## Hebrew Name Note

> הם מאמינים שבתי הדרקון התחזקו יתר על המידה. הפתרון שלהם: להתחזק בעצמם.
> האורום לא נלחם במלחמות. הם קונים את האנשים שנלחמים.
