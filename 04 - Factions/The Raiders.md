# The Raiders

## Overview
[[Captain Ash]]'s crew — a mix of war veterans, disillusioned idealists, and desperate survivors.
They are not terrorists. They are not thieves. They are soldiers who lost a war and refuse to lose the peace.

## Structure
- **[[Captain Ash]]** — leader, strategist, moral compass (broken but functional)
- **First Mate Kael** — [[Captain Ash|Ash]]'s second; a Cyran refugee who lost everything
- **The Twins (Mira and Voss)** — scouts and saboteurs; communicate in a coded twin-language
- **Doc Hallow** — field medic; keeps the crew alive; knows too much about chemical weapons
- **Six to ten rotating members** — veterans, deserters, radicals

## Philosophy
"The Houses are the new nations. And we are still at war."
They steal from the Houses to survive. They expose House crimes because someone has to.
They are slowly becoming the very thing they hate — organized, armed, and willing to kill.

## Role in the Campaign
- Immediate antagonists who become potential allies
- If [[Captain Ash|Ash]] survives, the Raiders become a faction the players can work with
- If [[Captain Ash|Ash]] dies, the Raiders fracture — some become terrorists, some surrender, some hunt the players

> הם לא טרוריסטים. הם לא גנבים. הם חיילים שאיבדו מלחמה ומסרבים לאבד את השלום.
