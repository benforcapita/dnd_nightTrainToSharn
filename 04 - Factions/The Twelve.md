# The Twelve

## Overview
The arcane research arm of the dragonmarked houses.
Officially: a council of mages advancing magical knowledge for the good of [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]].
Actually: a shadow government conducting experiments that would horrify the nations if exposed.

## Philosophy
"Magic is progress. Progress requires sacrifice."
They do not think of themselves as villains. They think of themselves as surgeons saving a patient — and the patient is [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]]. The anesthesia is everyone else's ignorance.

## Role in the Campaign
- Authorized [[The Statue]] transport
- Want the shard for research — possibly to replicate or weaponize its properties
- Have agents everywhere, including on [[The Firefly]]
- If they learn the players have the shard, they will become the campaign's most dangerous enemy

## Key Members
- **[[Future Villains|Cipher]]** — anonymous operative; possibly multiple people
- **Arcanist Velira** — obsessed with [[The Statue|Dhakaani]] binding magic
- **The Chamber of Eyes** — surveillance division; they are always watching
