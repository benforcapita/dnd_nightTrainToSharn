# [[The Firefly]] Incident

## What Happened (Official Version)
"On the evening of [date], terrorist sympathizers attempted to sabotage the maiden voyage of [[The Firefly]] lightning rail.
[[House Deneith]] security forces successfully repelled the attack.
[[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]] ir'Orien]] demonstrated exceptional leadership under fire.
No casualties among civilian VIPs. Minimal property damage.
[[The Firefly]] arrived at [[Terminus Station]], [[Sharn]], only slightly behind schedule."

**Every word is a lie.**

## What Really Happened
- [[Captain Ash]] and his crew boarded the train mid-journey
- They fought Deneith security to reach the cargo car
- [[The Statue]]'s seal was damaged in the fighting
- [[The Siberys Shard]] was exposed
- In the chaos, the players took possession of the shard
- [[Captain Ash|Ash]] escaped, was captured, or died
- At least three guards and two [[The Raiders|Raiders]] died
- [[Professor Nymm]] vanished during the confusion

## The Cover-Up
[[House Orien]] is burying the story.
- Bribes to newspapers
- Threats to witnesses
- Deneith guards ordered to sign non-disclosure agreements
- [[The Statue]] officially "never existed"
- The shard officially "was never on the train"

## Why It Matters
If the truth comes out:
- [[House Orien]]'s reputation is damaged
- [[The Twelve]]'s involvement is exposed
- The existence of the shard becomes public knowledge
- Every faction in [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]] will descend on the players

The players are the only ones who know the full truth.
That makes them powerful.
That makes them targets.
