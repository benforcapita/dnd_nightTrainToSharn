# The Statue

## Overview
An eight-foot Dhakaani stone sentinel, carved from black basalt.
Its face is serene. Its hands are pressed together in prayer — or binding.
It is older than Galifar. Older than the Five Nations.
And it was not built to be beautiful. It was built to *contain*.

## Physical Description
- Black basalt, impossibly smooth for stone so ancient
- Eyes closed, lips curved in a slight, knowing smile
- Armor carved in the Dhakaani style — overlapping scales, no insignia
- Hands pressed together across the chest, where the crack now pulses
- Base inscribed with Dhakaani runes: *"The Watcher Dreams. The Dreamer Watches."*

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> שומר אבן דהאקאני בגובה שמונה רגל, מגולף מבזלת שחורה. פניו שקטות. ידיו לחוצות יחד בתפילה — או כבליה. הוא עתיק יותר מגליפאר. יותר מחמש האומות. והוא לא נבנה להיות יפה. הוא נבנה *להכיל*.

## The Hidden Compartment
The statue's chest is hollow — not by chance, by design.
The compartment was sealed with a combination of Dhakaani binding magic and modern House Cannith arcane locks.
When the seal fails, the compartment opens like a flower of stone.
Inside: [[The Siberys Shard]].

## True Purpose
The Dhakaani did not worship the dragonshards. They feared them.
This statue is one of many *sentinels* placed across [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]] — each containing a fragment of something larger.
The Dhakaani believed that if the fragments were reunited, something terrible would wake.
They were right.

## DM Notes
- Let players study the statue. Let them feel its age.
- [[Professor Nymm]] knows some of this. He is afraid to say it aloud.
- The runes are in Old Dhakaani — DC 20 History or DC 18 with [[Professor Nymm|Nymm]|Nymm]]'s help
- If the shard is removed, the statue becomes inert... but not dead. It is watching.
