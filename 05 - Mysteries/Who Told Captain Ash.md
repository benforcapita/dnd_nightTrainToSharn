# Who Told [[Captain Ash]]?

## The Mystery
[[Captain Ash|Ash]] knew [[The Firefly]] was carrying something important.
He knew which car. He knew the approximate timing.
He did not know about [[The Siberys Shard]] specifically — only that [[House Orien]] was moving "something dangerous."

Someone inside — or connected to — [[House Orien]] leaked this information.
Who? And why?

## Suspects

### 1. [[The Aurum]] (Most Likely)
- They want chaos. Chaos creates opportunity.
- They tipped off [[Captain Ash|Ash]] hoping he would steal [[The Statue]], then they would steal it from him.
- They did not expect the players to end up with it.

### 2. A Rival Within [[House Orien]]
- Someone who wants [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]] to fail.
- [[The Firefly]] is her test. If it goes wrong, she is disgraced.
- This person may be working with [[The Twelve]] or acting alone.

### 3. [[The Twelve]] Directly
- They wanted the transport exposed to test security.
- Or they wanted an excuse to eliminate [[Captain Ash|Ash]]'s crew as a nuisance.
- Either way, the players are now variables they did not predict.

### 4. [[Professor Nymm]] (Unlikely but Possible)
- He has contacts everywhere.
- He might have mentioned the transport to the wrong person.
- He is too scattered to be malicious, but not too scattered to be dangerous.

### 5. [[Future Villains|Cipher]]
- The anonymous Twelve operative.
- If [[Future Villains|Cipher]] exists, they are playing a game the players cannot see yet.

## Campaign Implications
- Finding the leak is a major plot thread for Arc 2 ([[Sharn]] Noir)
- The leak may become an ally, a villain, or a corpse
- Whoever it is, they are still out there. And they know the players have the shard.
