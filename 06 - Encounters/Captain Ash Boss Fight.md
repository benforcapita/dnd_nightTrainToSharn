# [[Captain Ash]] Boss Fight

## Overview
[[Captain Ash|Ash]] is not a boss in the traditional sense. He is a moral challenge.
If the players engage him in combat, he fights to disable, not to kill — unless they give him no choice.
He respects soldiers. He despises house agents. He pities civilians caught in the crossfire.

## Combat Phases

1. **Round 1:** [[Captain Ash|Ash]] directs his crew to secure exits while he engages the most threatening player.
2. **Round 2-3:** Uses environment — train movement, debris, cover. Avoids direct confrontation with groups.
3. **Round 4+:** If outmatched, uses concussion grenades to create escape routes. He will not die for the shard — but he will die for his crew.

## Moral Victory Condition
The best outcome is not killing [[Captain Ash|Ash]]. It is convincing him the players are not his enemies — or that the Houses are not *their* enemies either.
If the players spare him or negotiate, he becomes a powerful (if complicated) ally.
