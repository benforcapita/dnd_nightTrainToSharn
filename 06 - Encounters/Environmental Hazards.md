# Environmental Hazards

## Moving Train
At initiative count 20 (losing ties), roll or choose one environmental effect.

## Effects Table (d6)

### 1. Sudden Turn
The train takes a sharp curve.
- **DC 13 Dexterity save** or fall prone
- Prone creatures slide `1d4 x 5 feet` toward the outer wall
- Failed save by 5+ = fall and take `1d6 bludgeoning damage`
- If near a connecting platform, risk falling between cars

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **פנייה פתאומית:** DC 13 Dexterity save או נופלים. יצורים על הקרקע מחליקים `1d4 x 5 רגל` לקיר החיצוני. כישלון ב-5+ = נפילה ו-`1d6` נזק מכה.

### 2. Lightning Surge
Arcane energy jumps between metal surfaces.
- Anyone touching metal takes `1d6 lightning damage`
- The lights die completely — dim emergency lamps only
- Metal weapons conduct: wielders take `1d4 lightning damage` on next hit
- Casters must make **DC 12 Con save** or lose concentration

> **Hebrew / עברית:**
> **התפרצות ברק:** מי שנוגע במתכת לוקח `1d6` נזק ברק. מנורות חירום בלבד. כלי מתכת מוליכים: מחזיקים לוקחים `1d4` נזק ברק במכה הבאה.

### 3. Flying Debris
Broken cargo shifts across the battlefield.
- `1d4` loose objects become projectiles at random initiative counts
- **DC 12 Dexterity save** or take `1d6 bludgeoning` from a crate/chair/tool
- Creates difficult terrain in `1d2` random 5-foot squares

### 4. Open Roof Gust
A hole in the roof or an open connecting platform creates suction.
- Any creature within 10 feet of an opening must make **DC 14 Strength save** or be pulled 10 feet toward the opening
- If pulled into the opening: **DC 15 Dexterity save** or fall from the train (`6d6 bludgeoning + 2d6 lightning`)
- Ranged attacks through the opening have disadvantage

### 5. Statue Collapse
If fighting in the cargo car: [[The Statue]] cracks further.
- Stone fragments create difficult terrain in a 10-foot radius
- Sharp shards: moving through the area requires **DC 12 Dexterity save** or take `1d4 slAshing`
- The shard's glow intensifies — all magical effects within 30 feet are amplified (+1 to spell attack rolls, +1 damage die)

### 6. Elemental Disruption
The bound elemental in the engine falters.
- The train slows suddenly — all creatures must make **DC 10 Dexterity save** or stumble (no mechanical effect, but next attack roll is at disadvantage)
- The temperature drops 20 degrees in one round
- Ghostly whispers in Primordial echo through the cars
