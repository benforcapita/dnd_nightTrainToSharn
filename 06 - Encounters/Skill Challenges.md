# Skill Challenges

## Moving Between Cars Under Fire
**Situation:** Players need to move from one car to another while combat rages or the train is unstable.
- **DC 12 Acrobatics** — maintain balance on the connecting platform
- **DC 14 Athletics** — climb along the exterior
- **DC 10 Perception** — spot handholds or safe paths
- **Failure by 5+:** Fall or take `1d6` damage from a hard landing
- **Three successes before three failures:** Reach the target car safely

## Swaying the Crowd
**Situation:** Players need to convince panicked passengers to take action (barricade doors, hide, etc.)
- **DC 13 Persuasion** — calm and organize
- **DC 12 Intimidation** — command obedience
- **DC 14 Insight** — identify which passengers will listen vs. which will panic
- **DC 15 Performance** — a rousing speech might work on some
- **Success:** Passengers become helpful or at least out of the way
- **Failure:** Panic spreads; someone gets hurt

## Disabling the Arcane Seal
**Situation:** The cargo car seal needs to be opened — by [[The Raiders|Raiders]], players, or both.
- **DC 16 Arcana** — understand the binding magic
- **DC 15 Thieves' Tools** — physically dismantle the mechanism
- **DC 14 History** — recognize [[The Statue|Dhakaani]] elements and their weaknesses
- **DC 13 Investigation** — find the focal point of the seal
- **Success:** Seal opens safely
- **Failure:** Seal triggers a backlash — `2d6` force damage in 10 ft. radius

## Navigating the Roof
**Situation:** Pursuit or escape across the train roof.
- **DC 14 Athletics** — maintain grip and speed
- **DC 12 Acrobatics** — dodge obstacles and gusts
- **DC 13 Perception** — spot threats ([[The Raiders|Raiders]], debris, incoming tunnels)
- **Natural 1:** Nearly fall — next check is at disadvantage
- **Three successes:** Traverse safely

## Aftermath Negotiation
**Situation:** After the battle, multiple parties want the shard. Players must navigate a tense standoff.
- **DC 15 Persuasion** — propose a compromise
- **DC 14 Deception** — bluff about having backup
- **DC 13 Insight** — read [[Captain Ash|Ash]]'s or [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]]'s true intentions
- **DC 16 Intimidation** — force a party to back down
- **Success:** Standoff resolves without further bloodshed
- **Failure:** Combat resumes. Possibly fatal for someone.
