# [[Sharn]] Ambitions

## Overview
Every character needs a reason to stay in [[Sharn]] after Session 1. The shard is not enough — they need personal stakes, dreams, and debts that tie them to the City of Towers.

## Possible Ambitions

### 1. The Clinic
**Concept:** You want to build or work at a veterans' clinic in Lower Dura. A place where broken soldiers can be broken together. Where no one asks why you flinch at loud noises.
**Connections:** [[Sharn|Morgrave University]] medical program; illegal warforged repair shops; House Jorasco (healing house, expensive)
**Conflict:** The clinic needs funding. [[The Aurum]] offers investment — with strings.

### 2. The Tavern
**Concept:** You want to open a tavern — neutral ground where veterans from all nations can drink without killing each other. You have a name already. The treaty. The peace. The Lie. You haven't decided.
**Connections:** The Broken Anvil (competition? inspiration?); local gangs demanding protection; a Cyran chef who makes food that tastes like memory

### 3. The Truth
**Concept:** You want to know what really ended the war. Not the treaties. The [[Timeline|Mourning]]. You believe the shard is a clue.
**Connections:** [[Sharn|Morgrave]] researchers; conspiracy theorists in [[Sharn|The Cogs]]; a dragon who answers questions with riddles
**Conflict:** Everyone who gets close to the truth seems to disappear.

### 4. The Family
**Concept:** You have family in [[Sharn]] — or you think you do. A sibling you haven't seen since the war. A child you didn't know existed. A parent who disowned you for enlisting.
**Connections:** Lower Dura neighborhoods; House Sivis message services; private investigators who specialize in wartime separations

### 5. The Debt
**Concept:** You owe someone money. A lot of money. Maybe a loan shark. Maybe a house. Maybe a friend who covered your expenses while you were too broken to work.
**Connections:** [[The Aurum]]'s financial networks; [[Sharn]]'s underworld; legitimate banks that won't touch veterans
**Conflict:** The debt is coming due. And the shard is suddenly worth more than your life.

### 6. The Craft
**Concept:** You are an artificer, musician, writer, or artisan. You want to make something beautiful in a city that trades in ugliness.
**Connections:** The Bazaar of Middle Dura; House Cannith's innovation labs; underground galleries in [[Sharn|The Cogs]]
**Conflict:** Beauty is dangerous in [[Sharn]]. It attracts attention. The wrong kind.

### 7. The Redemption
**Concept:** You did something terrible in the war. You want to balance the scales — not erase it, but outweigh it.
**Connections:** Religious orders; charitable organizations; individuals whose lives you destroyed
**Conflict:** Redemption is not a destination. It is a direction. And the path is steep.

## DM Guidelines
- Ask each player for their [[Sharn]] ambition before Session 1
- Tie these ambitions to the main plot — the shard affects everything
- Let players achieve their ambitions, but make them work for it
- An ambition achieved should create a new ambition
