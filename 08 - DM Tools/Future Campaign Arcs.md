# Future Campaign Arcs

## Arc 1 — [[The Firefly]] Incident
The players survive the heist and hide the shard.
They arrive in [[Sharn]] as nobodies with a secret.
This arc establishes the tone, the world, and the central mystery.
**Status:** Complete after Session 1.

## Arc 2 — [[Sharn]] Noir
Factions begin searching for the missing shard.
The party becomes entangled with:
- Criminal syndicates (the Boromar Clan, the Tyrants)
- Dragonmarked houses (Orien, Deneith, Cannith, Sivis)
- [[Sharn|Morgrave University]] ([[Professor Nymm]]'s contacts, rival scholars)
- Spies (Thranish intelligence, Cyran survivors, House Phiarlan)

**Key Events:**
- A break-in at the players' lodgings
- An offer from [[The Aurum]] they can't refuse
- A [[Sharn|Morgrave]] researcher who knows too much — and dies for it
- [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]] reappears, either as ally or hunter

**Status:** Sessions 2-6

## Arc 3 — Ancient Secrets
The shard connects to forgotten [[The Statue|Dhakaani]] ruins beneath [[Sharn]] — and beyond.
The players discover:
- [[The Statue]] was one of many sentinels
- The [[The Statue|Dhakaani]] were not destroyed by the daelkyr alone
- Something worse sleeps beneath [[Khorvaire Interactive Map|Khorvaire]]
- The shard is a key — but to what?

**Key Events:**
- Expedition to [[Sharn|The Cogs]]
- Contact with the Keeper of [[Sharn|The Cogs]]
- A dragon's interest — direct and terrifying
- The [[Future Villains|Lord of Blades]] begins hunting the players

**Status:** Sessions 7-12

## Arc 4 — The Next War
The players uncover evidence that powerful groups are preparing for another continental conflict.
- House Cannith is building warforged that do not need sleep or orders
- Karrnath is reactivating its undead legions
- [[The Twelve]] is consolidating power
- Someone — possibly [[The Aurum]], possibly [[The Twelve]] — wants the war to happen

**Key Events:**
- A massacre blamed on "rogue veterans"
- The players are framed or recruited
- A meeting of powers where the shard is demanded as tribute
- The choice: expose the conspiracy or use it for leverage

**Status:** Sessions 13-18

## Arc 5 — Prophecy
The shard is tied to a fragment of the [[Prophecy Fragments|Draconic Prophecy]].
The players learn:
- The [[Timeline|Mourning]] was not an accident — it was a *word* in the Prophecy
- The shard contains the next word
- If spoken, it could prevent the next [[Timeline|Mourning]]
- If spoken, it could cause it

**Key Events:**
- Travel to Argonnessen or Xen'drik (or both)
- A conversation with a dragon that changes everything
- The revelation of [[Future Villains|Cipher]]'s identity
- The final choice: speak the word, destroy the shard, or become its guardian

**Status:** Sessions 19-24 (Campaign finale)
