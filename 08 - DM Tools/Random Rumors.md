# Random Rumors

## How to Use
Roll d20 or choose. Some rumors are true. Some are partially true. Some are lies that become true because people believe them.

## Rumors About [[The Firefly]]
1. *"[[The Firefly]]'s conductor stones are bound with a forbidden elemental — not air, but something that fell from the sky."* **(False — but the stones ARE unstable)**
2. *"Lady [[Lady Elira ir'Orien|Elira] ir'Orien|Elira]] isn't Orien by blood. She's a foundling the patriarch took pity on."* **(False — but she is new money, not old blood)**
3. *"The cargo manifest lists 'archaeological specimens,' but the guards call it 'the package.' Everyone on the crew knows it's something else."* **(True)**
4. *"Marshal [[Marshal Serik ir'Deneith|Serik] ir'Deneith|Serik]] killed his own unit in the war. That's why he works for Deneith now — no one else would take him."* **(False — but he did lose his unit)**

## Rumors About [[Sharn]]
5. *"The City Watch found three bodies in [[Sharn|The Cogs]] last week. Each one had a dragonshard fragment embedded in their chest. Not placed. Grown."* **(Partially true — [[The Twelve]] is experimenting)**
6. *"The Broken Anvil Tavern is owned by a dragon. A real one. In halfling form."* **(False — but the owner is connected to the Boromar Clan)**
7. *"Someone's been buying up property near [[Sharn|Morgrave University]]. Paying triple market value. House Cannith, probably. Or worse."* **(True — [[The Aurum]])**
8. *"The sky towers aren't empty at the top. They're full of things that aren't human anymore."* **(False — probably)**

## Rumors About the [[Timeline|Last War]]
9. *"The [[Timeline|Mourning]] wasn't an accident. Cyre was experimenting with a weapon, and it turned on them."* **(Partially true — but who was experimenting?)**
10. *"Breland knew the [[Timeline|Mourning]] was coming. They pulled their troops from the border three days before."* **(False — but Breland DID have advance warning)**
11. *"There are warforged still fighting in the Mournland. They don't know the war ended."* **(True — and terrifying)**
12. *"Karrnath's undead soldiers aren't volunteers. They're deserters. Reanimated as punishment."* **(Partially true)**

## Rumors About the Shard
13. *"[[The Siberys shard|Siberys shard]s don't just enhance magic. They remember it. Every spell ever cast near one is recorded in the crystal."* **(Unknown — but [[Professor Nymm]] believes this)**
14. *"The dragons of Argonnessen have agents in every House. They're watching the shard. They're watching you."* **(Partially true)**
15. *"Someone in [[Sharn]] has already built a weapon using a [[The Siberys shard|Siberys shard]. It melted an entire district. The Watch covered it up."* **(False — but House Cannith has tried)**
16. *"The shard isn't a stone. It's an egg."* **(Almost certainly false — but what if?)**

## Rumors About the Players
17. *"Those veterans from [[The Firefly]]? [[House Orien]] bought their silence. Look at them — they're living too well for nobodies."* **(False — but the assumption is dangerous)**
18. *"I heard one of them was Cyran. Survived the [[Timeline|Mourning]]. You know what that means? They're cursed."* **(False — but superstition is powerful)**
19. *"They didn't survive the heist by luck. They knew it was coming. One of them is working with [[The Raiders]]."* **(False — unless a player took the Informant secret!)**
20. *"Ask them about [[The Statue]]. Watch their faces. They know something."* **(Dangerously true)**
