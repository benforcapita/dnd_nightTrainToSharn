## The Explosion — Quick Leads

- **Spellshard:** Contains map and partial message
- **Recording crystal:** Shows attacker with aberrant mark / changeling / daelkyr magic / elemental binding
- **Witnesses:** Shifter child, Boromar lookout, Phiarlan entertainer, dead civilian's letter
- **Cannith serial number:** Construct dispatched from Bazaar workshop, dispatch order forged

## Shard Progression

| Session | What the Shard Does |
|---|---|
| 1 | Humming, warm, alive-feeling |
| 2 | Dream of the Forge-Warden, pulses on entering Sharn |
| 3 | Warms near dragonmarks, hums near aberrant marks, cold near Dhakaani door |
| 4 | Reveals itself as a Key; projects vision of Duur'Sharaat; attunement possible |
| 5-8 | Guides toward Duur'Sharaat entrance; senses other Keys nearby; dreams intensify |
| 9-10 | Opens the vault; goes cold when schema is claimed/destroyed |
| 11+ | Becomes inert — or awakens fully, depending on Prophecy arc |

## The Schema — Delivery Options

| If Party Does This... | Then... |
|---|---|
| Sells to Merrix d'Cannith | 500,000 gp. Rich, famous, eternal targets. Merrix becomes patron or enemy. |
| Gives to a faction patron | That faction becomes dominant. The party is rewarded — and owned. |
| Destroys the schema | The shard goes cold. The Prophecy shifts. Everyone is furious. The dragons take notice. |
| Keeps the schema | The party becomes the most dangerous private faction in Khorvaire. Every faction either courts or hunts them. |
| Copies the schema, sells original | Partial payment. Partial knowledge. Partial danger. Everyone is partially satisfied — and partially betrayed. |
| Seals Duur'Sharaat permanently | The party becomes the new wardens. The Keeper of the Cogs acknowledges them. The schema is safe — for now. |
