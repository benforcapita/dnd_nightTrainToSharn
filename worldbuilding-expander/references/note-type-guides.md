# Note-Type Guides

Load this reference when the user is working on a specific note type and you need
detailed gap detection heuristics or template guidance.

## Region

**Already defined?** — Climate, borders, major terrain features.
**Often missing:**
- What resources exist and who extracts them?
- How do people travel across it? What are the actual routes and dangers?
- What former powers shaped its borders and why?
- What languages or dialects are spoken here?
- What does daily life look like in its sub-areas?
- Suggested linked notes: major settlements, factions with territorial claims,
  trade routes, natural hazards, historical battles.

## City / Settlement

**Already defined?** — Name, location, notable buildings.
**Often missing:**
- Who governs and how did they gain power?
- What do people eat and where does food come from?
- What are the class divisions and where do people live?
- What laws are enforced and who enforces them?
- What is the local economy, currency, and trade balance?
- How does waste, water, fire, and disease get managed?
- What taboos or customs shape public behavior?
- Suggested linked notes: ruling faction, neighborhoods, markets,
  temples, criminal underworld, nearby threats.

## Faction

**Already defined?** — Name, goals, leader.
**Often missing:**
- What pressure or problem created this faction?
- How does it fund itself?
- Who are the internal rivals or dissenters?
- What does membership cost or require?
- What do outsiders get wrong about it?
- What has it sacrificed to get where it is?
- Suggested linked notes: leader NPC, rival factions,
  territories controlled, key events in its history.

## Religion

**Already defined?** — Deities, holy texts, domains.
**Often missing:**
- What do adherents actually do day to day?
- What rituals mark birth, marriage, death, and crisis?
- Who holds religious authority and how is it challenged?
- How does this religion interact with political power?
- What heresies exist and how are they treated?
- What miracles or effects do believers claim?
- What does the religion prohibit and what happens to violators?
- Suggested linked notes: holy sites, religious NPCs,
  heretical branches, religious laws, linked factions.

## History / Event

**Already defined?** — What happened, major figures involved.
**Often missing:**
- What were the immediate causes, not just proximate triggers?
- Who benefited and who suffered?
- How do different factions remember it differently?
- What documents or evidence survive?
- What did this event make possible or impossible afterward?
- Are there living witnesses or descendants with stakes?
- Suggested linked notes: participating factions, key NPCs,
  locations where it unfolded, later consequences.

## Magic System

**Already defined?** — Source, mechanics, user types.
**Often missing:**
- What does magic cost the user or the world?
- Who controls access and how?
- What social effects does magic create?
- How does it interact with religion, law, and military?
- What happens when magic goes wrong?
- What innovations or forbidden techniques exist?
- Suggested linked notes: magical institutions, notable mages,
  magical locations, historical magical disasters.

## Culture

**Already defined?** — Customs, dress, art.
**Often missing:**
- How is identity formed and transmitted?
- What foods, games, and songs define ordinary life?
- What are the kinship and inheritance rules?
- What do they fear and what do they celebrate?
- How do they treat outsiders, elders, and the dead?
- What language quirks, naming patterns, or oral traditions exist?
- Suggested linked notes: ancestral region, religious practices,
  diaspora communities, cultural NPCs.

## Character / NPC

**Already defined?** — Name, role, appearance.
**Often missing:**
- What do they want and what do they fear?
- What obligations tie them to factions or places?
- What secrets do they keep and who knows them?
- How do they speak, dress, and behave in private vs. public?
- What do they actually do day to day?
- Who depends on them and who resents them?
- Suggested linked notes: faction allegiance, home location,
  rivals, family, key events they participated in.

## Conflict / Quest

**Already defined?** — Stakes, sides, desired outcome.
**Often missing:**
- What caused this conflict to ignite now?
- What do both sides believe about the other?
- What resources can each side actually deploy?
- What would a victory or defeat look like in practice?
- What third parties might intervene?
- What past grievances fuel the present fight?
- Suggested linked notes: involved factions, key NPCs on each side,
  locations of confrontation, historical antecedents.

## Location / Dungeon

**Already defined?** — Physical description, inhabitants.
**Often missing:**
- Who built it and why?
- How has it changed hands over time?
- What supplies and logistics sustain inhabitants?
- What secrets remain undiscovered and why?
- How does it connect to the broader region or underworld?
- What local legends or warnings exist?
- Suggested linked notes: builders, current occupants,
  nearby settlements, connected locations, historical events.

## [[Timeline]] / Era

**Already defined?** — Dates, major events in sequence.
**Often missing:**
- What living conditions were like for ordinary people?
- What technologies, magics, or beliefs rose or fell?
- What migrations, plagues, or climate shifts occurred?
- How did power structures rearrange?
- What do people from later eras get wrong about this time?
- Suggested linked notes: major events, dominant factions,
  influential NPCs, regions in their era-specific forms.
